## Quick Reference: Core Techniques

### 1. Front-Load Competence & Normalcy
Start with characters succeeding, living normal lives. Make readers invest before disruption.

### 2. Soft-Sell Technique
Show technology/systems as cool/useful/beautiful BEFORE revealing darker implications.

### 3. Sprinkle Hope Throughout
Don't save all warmth for the end. Humor, beauty, small victories even as darkness grows.

### 4. Save Deepest Despair
Build gradually. "All is lost" moment (Ch9) earns power by coming after hope and small wins.

### 5. Give Wins Before Losses
Show people succeeding before those things are taken away. Emotional contrast = investment.
